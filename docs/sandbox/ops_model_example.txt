  - steward_organization: ASCII_Short_String_Collapsed [0..*]

  - stewardship_note: ASCII_Text [0..1]

  - product_representation_type: ASCII_Short_String_Collapsed [0..1]

    - compressed --- The access endpoint provides a compressed representation.
    - cloud-native --- The access endpoint provides a cloud-native representation.
    - alternate --- The access endpoint provides an alternate representation.
		- primary --- The access endpoint provides the primary representation.

Lifecycle_Status --- The class Lifecycle_Status captures the current operational state of a product within the Engineering Node operational ingest. The lifecycle phase identifies where the product is in the workflow sequence, while the lifecycle status identifies the state of activity within that phase.

  - lifecycle_phase: ASCII_Short_String_Collapsed [0..1]
    Permissible values:
    - preparation --- The product is being planned, generated, documented, packaged, or otherwise prepared for archive or operational handling.
    - ingest --- The product is being transferred, harvested, loaded, registered, or accepted into an operational system.
    - review --- The product is undergoing operational, technical, peer, or approval review.
    - release --- The product is being prepared for or made available through an operational release workflow.

  - lifecycle_status: ASCII_Short_String_Collapsed [0..1]
    Permissible values:
    - pending --- The lifecycle phase has not yet started.
    - in_progress --- The lifecycle phase is currently underway.
    - blocked --- The lifecycle phase cannot proceed until an issue, dependency, or decision is resolved.
    - complete --- The lifecycle phase has been completed.
    - failed --- The lifecycle phase did not complete successfully.

  - status_date_time: ASCII_Date_Time [0..1]

  - status_message: ASCII_Text_Preserved [0..1]

Archive_Evaluation --- The class Archive_Evaluation captures the current evaluation state and quality assessment of a product, collection, or bundle within the PDS archival review and curation process. 

  - archive_evaluation_state: ASCII_Short_String_Collapsed [0..1]
    Permissible values:
    - not_evaluated --- The product has not yet entered the evaluation process.
    - in_review --- The product is currently undergoing review.
    - in_lien_resolution --- Review has identified issues or liens that are being resolved.
    - archived -- The product has successfully completed archive evaluation and is available through the archive.
    - rejected -- The product did not satisfy requirements for archival acceptance.

  - archive_quality: ASCII_Short_String_Collapsed [0..1]
    Permissible values:
    - certified --- The product represents the preferred or best available archive version.
    - acceptable --- The product satisfies archive quality requirements.
    - needs_improvement --- The product requires additional work before meeting archive quality requirements.
    - not_archive_quality --- The product is not intended to meet archive quality requirements.
    - undetermined --- Archive quality has not yet been determined.

	- accumulating_data_flag: ASCII_Boolean [0..1]
    --- Indicates whether additional data are still accumulating, such that some parts may be in the indicated archive curation state while other parts have not yet been delivered or have not progressed to the same state.

  - evaluation_date_time: ASCII_Date_Time [0..1]
    --- The date and time at which the archive evaluation was assigned or updated.

  - evaluation_message: ASCII_Text_Preserved [0..1]
    --- Free-text notes describing archive evaluation.

Validation_Assessment --- The class Validation_Assessment captures the result of validating a product against a specified information model, schema, schematron, local data dictionary, or validation tool.

  - validation_state: ASCII_Short_String_Collapsed [0..1]
    Permissible values:
    - passed --- The product passed the validation assessment.
    - failed --- The product failed the validation assessment.
    - passed_with_warnings --- The product passed validation but produced one or more warnings.
    - not_tested --- The product has not been validated.
    - unknown --- The validation result is not known.

  - validated_against_im_version: ASCII_Short_String_Collapsed [0..1]
    The version of the information model against which the product was validated.

  - validator_name: ASCII_Short_String_Collapsed [0..1]
    The name of the validation tool, service, or application used to perform the validation assessment.

  - validator_version: ASCII_Short_String_Collapsed [0..1]
    The version of the validation tool, service, or application used to perform the validation assessment.

  - validation_date_time: ASCII_Date_Time [0..1]
    The date and time at which the validation assessment was performed.

  - validation_message_text: ASCII_Text_Preserved [0..*]
    A human-readable description of a validation result, warning, or error generated during validation.

  - action_required: ASCII_Boolean [0..1]
    Indicates whether the validation assessment identified issues requiring corrective action.
