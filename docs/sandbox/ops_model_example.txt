Operation_Metadata --- The class Operation_Metadata is the top-level class for operational and supplemental metadata associated with a PDS4 product.
  - stewardship_info: Stewardship_Info [0..1]
  - label_file_info: File_Info [0..1]
  - data_file_info: File_Info [0..*]
  - access_info: Access_Info [0..*]
  - processing_provenance: Processing_Provenance [0..1]
  - lifecycle_status: Lifecycle_Status [0..*]
  - validation_assessment: Validation_Assessment [0..*]

Stewardship_Info --- The class Stewardship_Info describes the organizations responsible for producing and stewarding a product.

  - producer: ASCII_Short_String_Collapsed [0..*]
    --- The organization or entity that produced the product.

  - steward: ASCII_Short_String_Collapsed [0..*]
    --- The organization or entity responsible for stewardship, curation, or long-term management.

  - steward_type: ASCII_Short_String_Collapsed [0..*]
    Permissible values:
    - pds_node --- A PDS discipline or support node.
    - organization --- A non-PDS organization or institution.
    - agency --- A government or space agency.

  - stewardship_note: ASCII_Text [0..1]
    --- Free-text notes describing stewardship responsibilities, transfers, shared stewardship, or other relevant context.

Access_Info --- The class Access_Info describes an operational access endpoint or representation associated with a PDS4 product or file.
  - access_uri: ASCII_AnyURI [0..1]
  - access_method: ASCII_Short_String_Collapsed [0..1]
  	Permissible values:
  	-- https --- The access method uses HTTPS.
  	-- s3 --- The access method uses an Amazon S3 URI or compatible object-storage URI.
  	-- native --- The access method uses a native or system-local path.
  	-- internal --- The access method is intended for internal operational use.
  
  - product_representation_type: ASCII_Short_String_Collapsed [0..1]
    Permissible values:
    - browse --- The access endpoint provides a browse or preview representation.
    - download --- The access endpoint provides a downloadable representation.
    - compressed --- The access endpoint provides a compressed representation.
    - cloud-native --- The access endpoint provides a cloud-native representation.
    - alternate --- The access endpoint provides an alternate representation.
		- primary --- The access endpoint provides the primary representation.

  - is_user_visible: ASCII_Boolean [0..1]
  - authorization_required: ASCII_Boolean [0..1]
  - access_note: ASCII_Text_Preserved [0..1]

Lifecycle_Status --- The class Lifecycle_Status captures the current operational state of a product within its lifecycle. The lifecycle phase identifies where the product is in the workflow sequence, while the lifecycle status identifies the state of activity within that phase.

  - lifecycle_phase: ASCII_Short_String_Collapsed [0..1]
    Permissible values:
    - preparation --- The product is being planned, generated, documented, packaged, or otherwise prepared for archive or operational handling.
    - ingest --- The product is being transferred, harvested, loaded, registered, or accepted into an operational system.
    - review --- The product is undergoing operational, technical, peer, or approval review.
    - release --- The product is being prepared for or made available through an operational release workflow.


  - lifecycle_status: ASCII_Short_String_Collapsed [0..1]
    Permissible values:
    - pending --- The lifecycle phase has not yet started.
    - in_progress --- The lifecycle phase is currently underway.
    - blocked --- The lifecycle phase cannot proceed until an issue, dependency, or decision is resolved.
    - complete --- The lifecycle phase has been completed.
    - failed --- The lifecycle phase did not complete successfully.
    - superseded --- The product has been replaced by a newer version, successor, or operational replacement.

  - status_date_time: ASCII_Date_Time [0..1]

  - status_message: ASCII_Text_Preserved [0..1]

Validation_Assessment --- The class Validation_Assessment captures the result of validating a product against a specified information model, schema, schematron, local data dictionary, or validation tool.

  - validation_state: ASCII_Short_String_Collapsed [0..1]
    Permissible values:
    - passed --- The product or resource passed the validation assessment.
    - failed --- The product or resource failed the validation assessment.
    - passed_with_warnings --- The product or resource passed validation but produced one or more warnings.
    - not_tested --- The product or resource has not been validated.
    - unknown --- The validation result is not known.

  - validated_against_im_version: ASCII_Short_String_Collapsed [0..1]
    The version of the information model against which the product or resource was validated.

  - validated_against_ldd_version: ASCII_Short_String_Collapsed [0..*]
    The version(s) of any local data dictionaries against which the product or resource was validated.

  - validator_name: ASCII_Short_String_Collapsed [0..1]
    The name of the validation tool, service, or application used to perform the validation assessment.

  - validator_version: ASCII_Short_String_Collapsed [0..1]
    The version of the validation tool, service, or application used to perform the validation assessment.

  - validation_date_time: ASCII_Date_Time [0..1]
    The date and time at which the validation assessment was performed.

  - validation_message_text: ASCII_Text_Preserved [0..*]
    A human-readable description of a validation result, warning, or error generated during validation.

  - action_required: ASCII_Boolean [0..1]
    Indicates whether the validation assessment identified issues requiring corrective action.
